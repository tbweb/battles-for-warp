Class Motor

This class is the representation of the moving ability of a ship
-_speed		:	the base distance that can be traveled by the ship
-_boost		:	the bonus speed gave by the PP during the controll turn of the game
-_manover	:	the minimum traveling distance needed before stoping ship of rotate him
-_manovering:	boolean saying if the ship is in a manover

Getters and setters of the previous atributs :
+set_speed($s)
+set_manover($m)
+set_manovering($m)
+get_speed()
+get_manover()
+get_manovering()
